Migraine:
A severe headache usually felt on the side or front of the head. Migraine headaches often have other symptoms such as nausea, vomiting or increased sensitivity to light, sound or smells.

Have you already seen a doctor about these symptoms?

Why are we asking this?

This means that an assessment of your symptoms has been made by a GP or a hospital specialist.
