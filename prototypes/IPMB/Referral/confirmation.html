Worsening and self care advice

If there are any new symptoms, or if the condition gets worse, changes or you have any other concerns, call 111.

Self care

Unless advised not to, or they have already been given, paracetamol or ibuprofen can be used to relieve a headache. Follow the instructions in the pack. If in doubt ask a pharmacist.

Relaxation e.g. gentle exercise, a massage of the shoulders and neck muscles, a warm bath or simply a break from normal daily routine may help

Put a warm flannel on the back of the neck.
