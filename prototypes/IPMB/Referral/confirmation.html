What to do now

Looking after yourself

Paracetamol or ibuprofen can be used to relieve a headache. But, don't take them if you've been told not to, or you've already taken some. Read the instructions on the packet, or talk to a pharmacist for advice.

Try gentle exercise, a neck and shoulder massage, a warm bath or a break from your daily routine to help with a headache.

Place a warm flannel on the back of your neck.

If you get worse

If you have any new symptoms, or the condition gets worse, changes or you have any other concerns call 111.

If you haven't heard from us within 2 hours

Please call NHS 111 to rearrange your appointment.
