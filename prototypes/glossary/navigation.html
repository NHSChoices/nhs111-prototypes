Are you feeling severely ill and you also have new marks under your skin that look like bruising or bleeding?

Bruising:
To develop or bear a discolored spot on the skin as the result of a blow, fall, etc.
Bleeding:
The act, fact, or process of losing blood or having blood flow.

Why are we asking this?

We're looking for symptoms of meningitis or other serious illness. You'll feel that something is very seriously wrong with you. The illness will stop you from thinking about or doing anything else.

Meningitis:
The act, fact, or process of losing blood or having blood flow.

What does this mean?

We are looking for a new rash but only if you are extremely unwell. There may be small red-blue, purple or brown marks, pinprick spots or larger purple blotches or even blood blisters. On dark skin it is easier to see in paler areas, such as the mouth, palms and soles.
