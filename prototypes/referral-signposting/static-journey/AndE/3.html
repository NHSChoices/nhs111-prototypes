Do you have severe muscle aches, twitches or cramps?

Why are we asking this?

We need to know if you've been seriously affected by heat exposure.

What does this mean?

This means severe muscle aches, twitching or cramps that go on for more than a few minutes.
We don't mean the normal muscle aches and pains that people get after exercise.
